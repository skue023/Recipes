Hamburger

Duration: 20min

Ingredients
  1. ½ pounds of lean ground beef
  2. ½ onion, finely chopped
  3. ½ cup shredded Colby Jack or Cheddar cheese
  4. 1 large egg
  5. 1 ounce envelope dry onion soup mix
  6. 1 mincedclove garlic
  7. 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  8. 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  9. 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  10. 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  11. 1 teaspoon dried basil
  12. 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  13. ½ teaspoon crushed dried rosemary
  14. A pinch of salt and pepper or to taste
Steps:
  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  2. Meanwhile, combine ground beef, onion, cheese, egg, onion soup mix, minced garlic, garlic powder, soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, parsley, basil, oregano, rosemary, salt, and pepper in a large bowl.
  3. Use your hands to form the mixture into 4 patties.
  4. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
